Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Beisa Translate does not collect, sell, or share your translation content. Translation runs on your device.

On-Device Processing

Text, voice, and camera translation are processed locally on your iPhone. Translation content is not sent to Beisa servers.

Network Usage

Network access is used for optional model downloads and App Store system services. Beisa Translate does not include analytics, ads, or third-party tracking.

Camera & Microphone

Camera and microphone access are used for camera and voice translation when you choose those features. Inputs are processed locally and are not collected by Beisa.
